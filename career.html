Junior Data Analyst

For this position, we are seeking ambitious individuals intrigued by the realm of finance and quantitative analysis. Your prior experience doesn't hold significant weight for us; instead, we value your soft skills. In particular, a mathematical mindset and a fondness for formulas, dedication, a desire to dive deeply and thoroughly into a topic, and the capability to identify appropriate methodologies for analyzing vast quantities of data are crucial for this role.

Benefits

  • Kickstarting a career as a Quant Analyst – the most trendy and in-demand profession within the investment sector.
  • An interesting job that constantly presents new challenges, fostering your professional development.
  • Working at the forefront of current trends, enabling the amalgamation of fundamental mathematical knowledge and cutting-edge technologies.;

Skills and Qualifications

  • Higher education in Physics, Mathematics, or Economics is required. Our ideal
    candidate is either a senior student in these disciplines or a recent graduate.
  • Strong comprehension of Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability Theory, Mathematical
    Statistics, and Differential Equations is essential.
  • Basic proficiency in Python programming is necessary.
  • English language proficiency of level B1 or higher is required.
  • The capability to rapidly analyze voluminous information is essential.
  • A strong aspiration to excel in financial data analytics.
  • Perseverance in achieving objectives despite facing challenges.

Responsibilities

  • Review literature, lectures, and webinars from leading experts on quantitative trading.
  • Prepare analytical data and present it to the team for discussion.
  • Participation in the development of trading strategies.
  • Contribute to the development of neural network architectures.

Conditions

  • Remote work opportunity (full-time).
  • Stable salary (dependent on the applicant's skills and subject to individual discussion).
