What is Quant Trading?

Quantitative trading, often abbreviated as quant trading, is an investment strategy that relies on mathematical computations and data crunching to identify trading opportunities in the financial markets. The foundational elements of quant trading are complex algorithms and mathematical models which help analyze a wide variety of data – historical and real-time.

At the heart of quant trading is the process of building robust and efficient models, capable of making predictions about future price movements of financial assets.

The result is an objective and systematic trading strategy that evaluates market conditions based on data, rather than human emotions or assumptions. It empowers us to execute trades at high speed and frequency, while minimizing the risk associated with human error.

This approach is particularly applicable to new and fast-paced crypto market, where decisions need to be made quickly and efficiently. Quantitative trading capitalizes on modern technological advancements, leveraging machine learning to analyze massive datasets, formulate predictions, and carry out trades.
